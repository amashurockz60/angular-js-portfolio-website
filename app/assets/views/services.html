The Process

identify

It all starts with clearly identifying a need. A need to communicate, inform, educate, sway opinion, or to sell. Then to address the need with an intelligent design and strategy. Having defined your goals, you can go forward with clarity to build an effective business or project.

research

This stage is all about gaining a fundamental understanding about how the industry works, identifying your competitors, what makes your business unique, and learning what works versus what has failed in the past. This is arguably the most important stage, as a deep understanding and thorough research will lead to a strong final product.

develop

Having the knowledge and research done in previous stages gives us the ability to accurately develop the form of the final product. The majority of work happens here, setting up infrastructures, coding, designing, strategizing and getting feedback.

refine

After the final form of the product is developed, we can then refine it. Everything from adjusting the color scheme to tweaking the text. We make a thorough run through of the project to make sure it communicates what we want.
